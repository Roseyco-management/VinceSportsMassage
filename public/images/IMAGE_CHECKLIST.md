## How to Get These Images

### From WordPress Media Library:
1. Go to vincesportsmassage.com/wp-admin
2. Media > Library
3. Click each image
4. Copy the "File URL" on the right
5. Download and rename appropriately

### Or via FTP:
Download the entire `/wp-content/uploads/` folder from your hosting

### Or use a plugin:
Install "Export Media Library" plugin to download all at once
